Welcome to Metaverse Windows Wallet step by step detailed tutorial

The Metaverse full-node wallet is a cross-platform wallet program that operates trustlessly. It currently supports the Windows, Linux and MacOSX operating systems. Before installing please make sure you have at least 40 GB of space available on your hard drive. The current size of the Wallet (as of October 2020) after downloading the blockchain data is approx. 21 GB as seen in the picture below.


Step 1

 Please navigate to Metaverse main page located at https://mvs.org/



Step 2

 Scroll down to the bottom of the page and click the Download Wallet button



Step 3

 From the newly opened page choose the Desktop icon



Step 4

 And click on the Windows Wallet download link (red arrow)



Step 5

 After the download screen opens choose to save the file as below



Step 6

 If everything goes well you should see the downloaded file on your computer after few seconds. If the downloading process is slow just wait for the file to download



Step 7

 Click on the downloaded file. ATTENTION Very Important!!! Depending on your Windows version you will probably get a Warning screen like the one in the picture below. DO NOT PRESS OK. Instead press the “More info” text, where the yellow arrow is pointing on the image below
